# 九朝寻迹 | Nine Dynasties Quest
> **Revitalizing 3,000 years of history through AI-driven social storytelling.**


## Project Vision
Most cultural heritage sites are **"silent."** Visitors walk through museums without a connection to the past or each other. **Nine Dynasties Quest** turns historical sites into decentralized, interactive social games.

By bridging Top-tier Archaeological Research (Luoyang Institute) with Context-aware AI, we create an immersive "Nine Lives" reincarnation journey that fosters global cultural empathy.
